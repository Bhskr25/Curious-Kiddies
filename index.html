About Curious Kiddies

At Curious Kiddies Preschool and Day Care, we believe in fostering a philosophy rooted in the innate curiosity and wonder of children.

At Curious Kiddies Preschool and Day Care, we believe in fostering a philosophy rooted in the innate curiosity and wonder of children.

Nurturing Curiosity

We see curiosity as the driving force behind a child's learning journey. We provide a stimulating environment where children are encouraged explore, and discover the world around them leveraging their Innate Curiosity.

Holistic Development

We recognize that each child is unique and develops at their own pace. Our curriculum focuses on nurturing their Cognitive, Physical, Socia-emotional, and Language development of each child to help them reach their full potential.

Play-Based Learning

We believe that play is the primary way children learn. Through hands-on activities, imaginative play, and creative expression, children develop important skills such as problem-solving, critical thinking, and communication.

Respect for Individuality

We celebrate diversity and respect the individuality of each child. Our inclusive environment fosters a sense of belonging where children feel valued, accepted, and supported in their growth and development.

Partnership with Families

We believe that parents are a child's first and most important teachers. We strive to build strong partnerships with families, involving them in their child's learning journey and providing support and guidance every step of the way.

Lifelong Love for Learning

Our goal is to instill in children a lifelong love for learning. By nurturing their curiosity, creativity, and resilience, we empower children to become confident, independent learners who are prepared to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Why Choose Us ?

Nurturing Environment

Curious Kiddies Preschool and Day Care provides a nurturing environment where children feel safe, supported, and encouraged to Explore, Discover and Thrive.

Qualified Staff

Our team consists of experienced and Passionate educators and Nurturing Care takers dedicated to providing high-quality care and education to every child.

Activity-Based Learning

We believe in the power of play as a fundamental tool for learning and development. Our curriculum is designed to incorporate play-based activities that promote creativity, critical thinking, and social skills.

Focus on Individualized Learning

Every child is unique and has their own learning style and pace. Our programs are tailored to meet the individual needs and interests of each child, fostering their growth and development.

Engaging Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to be engaging, interactive, and age-appropriate, incorporating a variety of hands-on activities, arts and crafts, music, and outdoor play to keep children stimulated and excited about learning.

Community Engagement

We actively involve families in their child's learning journey and strive to build strong partnerships with parents through open communication, family events, and opportunities for involvement in the preschool community.

Safe and Secure Environment

The safety and well-being of children are our top priorities. We maintain a clean, secure, and child-friendly facility with strict safety protocols in place to ensure the utmost protection for all children under our care.

Preparation for School and Life

Our programs not only prepare children for academic success but also focus on developing important life skills such as independence, resilience, and empathy, laying a strong foundation for their future success and happiness.

At Curious Kiddies Preschool and Day Care, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities to enrich the learning experience and promote holistic development.

Music and Movement

Engaging music and movement sessions help children develop coordination, rhythm, and self-expression while having fun and staying active

Art and Craft

Creative art and craft activities encourage children to explore their imagination, develop fine motor skills, and express themselves through different artistic mediums.

Yoga and Mindfulness

We introduce children to basic yoga poses and mindfulness exercises to promote relaxation, concentration, and emotional well-being.

Outdoor Exploration

Outdoor play and nature walks provide opportunities for children to connect with the natural world, develop gross motor skills, and learn about the environment.

Language Enrichment

Language enrichment activities such as storytelling, puppet shows, and language games support literacy development and enhance communication skills.

Science Experiments

Hands-on science experiments and exploration activities spark curiosity and promote critical thinking skills as children observe, predict, and experiment with various scientific concepts.

Drama and Role-Play

Dramatic play and role-playing activities encourage imaginative thinking, social interaction, and language development as children engage in pretend play scenarios.
